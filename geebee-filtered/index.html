About Us

GeeBee is a leading Overseas Education Consultant that has assisted students in studying abroad at reputed Institutions in USA, UK, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, Germany, Singapore and more. With decades of experience and expertise under its belt, GeeBee has also assisted student with their Coaching for GRE, GMAT, IELTS, TOEFL & SAT. A solution-oriented company, which specializes in tailoring career pathways to suit individual profiles within any constraints that may prevail, GeeBee has time again proven to be the best choice for students wanting to pursue studies abroad.
